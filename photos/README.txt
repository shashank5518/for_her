Put your girlfriend's photos and videos here.

Photos: name them photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, etc. (or edit the paths in index.html to match your filenames)

Videos: add a video like this in the gallery-grid in index.html:
  <div class="gallery-item video-item">
    <video src="photos/yourvideo.mp4" muted loop playsinline></video>
  </div>
Use "muted" so autoplay works in browsers when she hovers or clicks.
